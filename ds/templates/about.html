About Us

Deserve Foundation was founded in January 2016 by Chantal Openda after the birth of her son who has congenital hydrocephalus. The distress and frustration she went through looking for treatment shed light to some of the challenges other people alike are going through.

The Team

Chantal Openda, Founder, Partnership

“What I went through with my son Taiden who has congenital hydrocephalus is not anything I would wish on anyone. The financial burden and emotional distress was too much to bear. My family were the only support system me and my son had. Through Deserve, I want to be family to others alike. I want to be a voice of HOPE to them because there is HOPE”

Chantal Openda is an experienced business developer and marketer. She has 6 years of experience working in B2B sales and marketing in the technology and business consulting industries.

Aside from her passion for children, she is an advocator for gender equality, keen on empowering the african girl. She is also a nature lover who has been involved in build a boma campaigns and save big cats.

Rosalia Lorenzen, Co-Founder, Operations and Finance and Funding

Rosalia is a highly motivated and driven Finance and Operations Director with over eight years’ experience in developing and managing financial systems, implementing sound internal controls, budgeting, financial accounting, tax compliance, risk mitigation, preparation of financial and Management reports, oversight of internal and external audits, business development human resource management, petty cash management, cash flow management and managing the day to day office operations.

She takes pride in working with and in organizations that focused on community development which aim at bringing change to our society.

Rosalia is self- driven, have a pleasant personality, great planning and organization skills and always embrace creative and innovative ways of thinking. She is very autonomous her work and have experience working in and with culturally diverse countries which include Tanzania, Mozambique, Nigeria, Cameroon, Senegal, DR Congo. Rosalia has an MBA in Finance from U.S. International University, she is a CPA-K and holds a First class Honors Degree in Finance and Accounting. At Deserve, she oversees the finance, operations and human resource management.
