Deserve Foundation is a movement of people from Kenya who believe that people born with congenital disorders DESERVE A RIGHT TO LIFE.

The Problem

There is little to no effort locally and across Africa to educate the public about congenital disorders. As a result, some families and individuals fall short to stereotypes and beliefs about the causes of some of these conditions. CURE Hospital, Uganda, a donor funded hospital in Uganda which specializes in neurosurgery and specifically hydrocephalus and spina bifida has some really shocking findings. In some villages in Uganda, families KILL their own children! Children who are born with Hydrocephalus and Spina Bifida are most often considered cursed or affected by witchcraft. There are cases where these children are abandoned or neglected and left to die in their homes. Eventually, these children succumb to the effects of non treatment and are secretly buried and forgotten about. In extreme cases, the women accompanied by their kin drown their own children in nearby rivers! Who in this case will fight for these children? Don't they DESERVE to live they life they have a chance of living?

Our Plan

Creating awareness

Knowledge is power. Deserve Foundation aims to educate the public about the causes and treatment of congenital disorders.

Lobbying

Persons with disabilities have the same rights as we do yet, they are the most neglected group in our society. We at Deserve aim to lobby for change in government policies not limited to, education, jobs, and health care for children and persons with diasbilities.

Sponsoring Treatment

Treatment which are mostly surgical are very expensive to self finance even for those who can afford it. Deserve foundation, through the help of our partner organizations and public donations will fund treatment for children coming from financially unstable families.

Fostering Support Groups

Anyone who has gone through a similar scenario or at the least had a loved one battling a disease knows the emotional weight this comes along with. These frustrations if not counselled lead to negative mental effects. Deserve Foundation through our network of volunteers and community based groups will foster support groups and counselling for Deserve families.
